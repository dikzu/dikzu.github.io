PRODUCTION

Pre-production for the film started in 2011 with Terry Rosio writing a script for the film. In early 2013, Jeff Nathanson was hired to write a new script with Depp being involved in Nathanson's writing process. Initially planned for a 2015 release, the film was pushed back to 2016 and then 2017, due to script and budget issues. Principal photography started in Australia in February 2015 after tbe Australian government offered Disney $20 million of tax incentives.
